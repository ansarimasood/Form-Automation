ABOUT US

The project aims to simplify the form filling process by
storing the required information of the user to a centralized
database. Different types of form templates will be available
in the system & at the time of registration user have to fill
all the details which will be used later. When user needs to
fill a form, only a unique ID will be needed and with that all
the information of the field will be retrieved from database
and the entire form will be filled automatically.Our aim is to
minimize the effort while filling the form by autofilling some details
of the forms like personal details, previous educational details etc.
User can change the information written in the field of the form
if they want.
